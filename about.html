About Maurifil LTD

Threading the future with sustainable dyeing solutions

Who We Are

Maurifil LTD is a leading yarn dyeing factory in Mauritius, known for our excellence in colour, quality, and service. Since our founding, we’ve built a reputation as a reliable partner in the textile industry, trusted by local and international clients.

What We Do

We specialize in commission-based package dyeing, with a fully equipped dyehouse and state-of-the-art laboratory. Our team provides precise colour matching and sustainable textile solutions tailored to your needs.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in sustainable yarn dyeing by combining innovation, eco-conscious practices, and unmatched service, contributing to Mauritius’s textile heritage.

Our Values

  • ✔ Quality in every thread
  • ✔ Fast and reliable service
  • ✔ Sustainable and eco-friendly processes
  • ✔ Building long-term client partnerships
